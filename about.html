The story behind the studio

We believe a home should feel composed rather than decorated, personal rather than performative, and enduring long after novelty has passed.

Harrington creates private residences defined by clarity, restraint and a lasting sense of atmosphere.

Founded in Manhattan in 2006, Harrington is a residential interior design studio recognised for an approach that is refined, measured and deeply attentive to the way a home is actually lived.

The studio was established by Eleanor Voss, whose work is guided by the conviction that beauty must be supported by purpose. With a background spanning architecture, interiors and the decorative arts, she founded Harrington as a practice grounded in proportion, material intelligence and the discipline of thoughtful design.

Rather than pursuing trend or visual excess, the studio develops interiors with a strong and lasting point of view. Each project begins with a close reading of the architecture, the context and the rhythms of daily life. From there, spaces are shaped with precision, allowing warmth, comfort and elegance to emerge with quiet confidence.

Harrington's portfolio includes landmark Manhattan apartments, Upper East Side residences, coastal properties, country estates and select international commissions. Though each project is distinct, all are united by the same principles: restraint, continuity, craftsmanship and a commitment to interiors that feel fully resolved.

Every commission is led with close creative oversight from the earliest design conversations through to the final installation. This continuity ensures that the original vision is preserved at every stage, resulting in homes that feel timeless, personal and quietly exceptional.
