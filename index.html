Our process

Listening

Every project begins with understanding. The space, the architecture, the client's rhythm of living. This phase defines direction before design begins.

Concept Development

Proportion, materiality and atmosphere are explored. Layouts are refined, palettes are composed, and the overall narrative takes form.

Design & Detailing

Drawings, specifications and bespoke solutions are developed with precision. Every junction, finish and layer is resolved with care.

Execution

Collaboration with craftsmen and suppliers ensures that the original vision remains intact. Clarity and oversight guide the project through completion.

Hi, I'm Melissa.

I founded this studio with a clear intention: to create homes that feel timeless, balanced and deeply personal. Over the years, I noticed how many spaces looked beautiful yet felt disconnected from the people living in them. I wanted to design differently, with greater care, clarity and purpose.

My work is guided by light, proportion and material. I focus on creating interiors that feel layered yet effortless, refined yet welcoming. Every decision is made with intention, ensuring that comfort and elegance exist in harmony.
